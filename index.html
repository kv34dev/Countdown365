Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026  ·  Version 1.0
TL;DR: My app collects absolutely no personal data. Everything stays on your device. I never see, store, or share anything about you.

1. Who I Am

I am an independent developer. Countdown is my personal iOS application designed to help users track time remaining until future events. This Privacy Policy explains my practices regarding any information related to your use of my app.

2. What Data I Collect

I collect no personal data whatsoever. My app does not have user accounts, registration, or sign-in of any kind. Specifically, I do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or any identifying information
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Analytics, crash reports, or usage metrics
  • Any data transmitted to my servers (I have none)
  • Payment or financial information

3. How I Store Your Data

All data my app saves — your timer names, target dates, selected theme — is stored exclusively in Apple's UserDefaults on your local device. This data never leaves your device through my app. It is never synced to any server I operate.

4. Third-Party Services

My app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or tracking tools. I use no external APIs or services within the app itself.

This website uses Formspree solely to receive support form submissions. Please review Formspree's own privacy policy if you choose to submit a request.

5. Children's Privacy

My app is suitable for all ages. Since I collect no personal data from anyone, there is no special concern regarding users under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR). My app complies with Apple's guidelines for apps available to all ages.

6. Your Rights

Since I collect no personal data, there is nothing for me to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. All your app data lives on your device and can be fully removed by uninstalling the app. If you have any concerns, I'm happy to address them through my support form below.

7. Changes to This Policy

If I ever update this Privacy Policy, I will post the revised version here with a new date. Continued use of my app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about my privacy practices, please reach out using my support form below. I typically respond within 2–3 business days.
